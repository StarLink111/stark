Managing your time during a pandemic
As you are entirely aware, pandemics, such as COVID-19, can completely turn your world upside down. What’s more, the uncertainty and break in normalcy can wreck all of the strides you made in managing your time. One of the most important things you should do is organize your plan for the day in order to be more productive,healthier and have fun at the same time.

 

Above this text, there are some icons showing things you should pay attetion to, and not just during this pandemic. Check them out !

Planning your day
Experts say establishing consistent daily and weekly routines is a way for people to feel there is some certainty during COVID-19 isolation. Among the advice for daily routines is to create a workspace outside your bedroom and get dressed as if you’re going into the office. Among the advice for weekly routines is to make your days consistent but still different, as well as selecting certain days for exercise and special dinners. While you should have a broad plan on how you’re going to spend your days, it also shouldn’t be too rigid that you don’t have any wiggle room. Remember, during uncertain times, things can change daily. And, since this is already a stressful time, you don’t want to add the pressure of following a strict schedule.
